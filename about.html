Books are a gateway

LITERATURE CAN BE A WINDOW into the world of someone else; opening the door to new experiences, thoughts, ideals, and perspective. A child who can access literature gains the key to discovery.

LITERACY AT HOME IS CRUCIAL for your child's reading development. So, what can you do with your child at home to help support their reading development and progress?.

Ideas

Idea 1

READ WITH YOUR CHILD! Allow them to ask questions, and ask questions as you are reading. As they engage in reading, they will become interested.

Idea 2

TAKE THEM TO THE LIBRARY. There they can have access to additional texts, and participate in additional literacy activities (such as reading challenges!)

Idea 3

LET THEM SEE YOU READ! Engage in reading simply by having famiy reading time and each read your own book!
