David Wieser

Wiesner's detailed and distinctive illustrations bring realism to the fantastical events and draw the reader in to experience something unexpected.

Guillaume Duprat

Duprat truly captivates readers with this nonfiction picturebook as he takes readers on a journeyto learn about how various animals of all types view the world. His use of use of soft lines and neutral colors gives the reader a sense of ease and calm, while the eye flaps add depth and texture to the story.

Chris Van Allsburg

Chris Van Allsburg’s exciting tale of a children’s board game coming to life will intrigue and captivate readers of all ages. Each page includes just enough features to draw the reader into each experience, but leave just enough out that the reader is able to add their own imagination. Readers will remain on the edge of their seats until the game’s competition.
